// 01 Unpack the Feature Break the big goal into clear, actionable pieces. Write it like an EPIC with smaller user stories. Add research “spikes” for unknown tools or workflows. Smaller tasks keep the momentum moving forward. // 02 Script the Success Write your outcome as if it’s already shipped. Use past tense: "Shipped v1.0 of my portfolio." Record and listen before sleep to prime your mindset. Your own voice can quiet the inner critic. // 03 Action Intentions = Sprint Plan Turn intent into scheduled, repeatable actions. Attach a clear definition of done to every task. Commit time blocks like you would for a sprint. Scheduled work ships; hopeful wishes don’t. // 04 Barrier Brainstorming List the obstacles most likely to appear. Write If/Then playbooks for each barrier. Have a backup plan for tech failures or time crunches. Preparedness keeps momentum when problems hit. // 05 Breathing Reset: 4-2-4 Inhale through the nose for 4 seconds. Hold for 2, then exhale through the mouth for 4. Repeat 3–5 times to reset your mental state. Use before high-focus coding or after stressful moments. // 06 Remember the Big Picture Pin your “why” somewhere visible in your workspace. Reframe negatives into learning statements. Keep a Wall of Wins: PRs, feedback, small launches. Vision keeps your work aligned and purposeful.
